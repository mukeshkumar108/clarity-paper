# Clarity Paper Product Evaluation Rubric

This rubric is used to evaluate the quality of the analysis produced by Clarity Paper.

## 1. Finding Extraction & Evidence (The "So What")
- [ ] Does it identify the core result of the study?
- [ ] Are the findings linked to direct quotes from the text?
- [ ] Does it explain the significance of the results in plain English?

## 2. Methodology & Reliability (The "How")
- [ ] Does it identify the study type (clinical trial, meta-analysis, etc.)?
- [ ] Does it find the sample size (n=X)?
- [ ] Does it check for a control group or randomization?
- [ ] Does it identify the duration of the study?

## 3. Caveats & Gaps (The "But")
- [ ] Does it flag potential funding bias or conflicts of interest?
- [ ] Does it identify limitations mentioned by the authors?
- [ ] Does it notice missing data or vague methodologies?
- [ ] Does it assign a confidence level (High/Medium/Low) that matches the paper's clarity?

## 4. Practical Utility (The "Now What")
- [ ] Does it provide clear, actionable takeaways for a regular person?
- [ ] Does it suggest intelligent follow-up questions for deeper investigation?
- [ ] Does it avoid making definitive medical claims?

## 5. Language & Hierarchy (The "Look")
- [ ] Is the language plain and accessible?
- [ ] Is the information organized from most important to least?
- [ ] Does the UI feel calm and focused on the evidence?
